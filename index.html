ABOUT

The University of New Brunswick was late to the game in establishing a varsity sailing team. Dalhousie, Memorial and other universities in the region have had established sailing teams for years, but the UNB Sailing Team was established only recently in 2017. In spite of still being in its infancy, the UNB team is comprised of very talented and skilled sailors, some of whom have had many years of competitive sailing experience. And the team has only experienced upwards growth since its inception.

Todd helped facilitate the first 2017 CICSA mid-winter regatta in Florida and has been a constant champion for collegiate sailing in Canada and the production of high-performance Canadian sailors.

Brandon Richardson

Director of Operations

I'll admit, my sailing experience is definitely lacking--especially when compared against others in the team. Prior to joining the UNB Sailing Team, I had roughly two years of casual keelboat sailing experience. The whole team has been incredibly receiving and encouraging despite this, which says a lot about the team and who can be a part of it. You don't need experience, you don't even need to participate in the competitive sailing! Just come have some fun, meet new people, and become part of a family.

Brandon is a third-year Software Engineering student. He is currently a member of the dingy racing team, and attends most keelboat races.
